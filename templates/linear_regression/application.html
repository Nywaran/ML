Linear Regression — Application

Predicting the closing price of gold using real market data.

Problem Description

This application uses a real dataset of gold futures to predict the closing price of gold based on historical market data. Gold is one of the most traded financial assets in the world, and being able to predict its price is valuable for investors and financial analysts.

Dataset

The dataset contains 1,169 daily records of gold futures from 2021 to 2024, including opening price, highest and lowest price of the day, trading volume, and technical indicators such as moving averages and RSI.

Variables Used

Independent variables (inputs): opening price, highest price, lowest price, and trading volume.
Dependent variable (output): closing price of gold (USD).
